The Culling (Early 2000s)

With the dawn of the internet, DVD-ROMs, and high definition televisions, a majority of companies left the console hardware market, leaving Sony, Nintendo, Sega, and Microsoft. Sega left the hardware production market in 2001 and many Sega of America employees became employed to work on the first Xbox.
